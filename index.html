Hornstrandir Trek — Iceland Westfjords, July 2026

Two hikers · 9 days · tent camping at designated sites · point-to-point Veiðileysufjörður → Hesteyri

★ Option A — recommended

9 hiking days. Direct ferry both ways from Ísafjörður. Three pickup-time choices on the final day.

Drop-off: Sat 11 Jul · Sjóferðir 09:00 · Ísafjörður → Veiðileysufjörður
Pickup: Mon 20 Jul · Borea 09:00 / 16:00 OR Sjóferðir 14:00 · Hesteyri → Ísafjörður

Day-by-day

Day 0 · Fri 10 Jul — Travel + Ísafjörður
Cologne → KEF → RKV → Ísafjörður · guesthouse

Buffer day for weather cancellations on the domestic flight. Visit Hornstrandastofa visitor centre (Silfurgata 1) for ranger conditions and pass snow status. Buy gas canisters at N1/Olís. Register travel plan at safetravel.is.

Day 1 · Sat 11 Jul — Veiðileysufjörður → Hornvík
11 km5–7 hHafnarskarð ~400 m

Sjóferðir 09:00 ferry to Veiðileysu. Cross Hafnarskarð (steep snow chute on north side mid-July). Camp at Hornvík (Höfn) — flush toilets, ranger station.

Day 2 · Sun 12 Jul — Hornbjarg cliff loop
~16 km6–7 hKálfatindar 534 m

Day-pack only. Puffins, gannets, fulmars on the dramatic edge. Camp Hornvík.

Day 3 · Mon 13 Jul — Hornvík → Hlöðuvík
14–15 km7–9 hAtlaskarð + Skálarkambur

Two passes, two river fords, "the heart of Hornstrandir" view. Camp Við Hlöðuvíkurós (pyramid dry toilet).

Day 4 · Tue 14 Jul — Rest at Hlöðuvík
flex

Coastal walk, Arctic fox spotting, swim at the black-sand beach. Side push to Búðir farm if energy is high.

Day 5 · Wed 15 Jul — Hlöðuvík → Hesteyri
14–15 km6–8 hKjaransvíkurskarð 426 m

Ford Kjaransvíkurá at the shoreline; another snowfield on descent. Camp Hesteyri — toilets, sink, ranger.

Day 6 · Thu 16 Jul — Hesteyri rest day
0–6 km

Doctor's House (Læknishúsið) cafe — waffles, soup, coffee. Walk to Stakkadalur whaling station ruins.

Day 7 · Fri 17 Jul — Side trip / weather buffer
flex

Sléttuhlíð side trail. Built-in slack for ferry weather cancellations.

Day 8 · Sat 18 Jul — Spare day
flex

Exploration around Hesteyrarfjörður OR pre-position via Aðalvík.

Day 9 · Sun 19 Jul — Final rest at Hesteyri
camp

No Sunday ferry to Ísafjörður — sit tight, last camp.

Day 10 · Mon 20 Jul — Ferry out
Borea / Sjóferðir

Pick from Borea 09:00 (earliest, fits same-day domestic flight), Borea 16:00, or Sjóferðir 14:00.

Option B — earlier finish

8 hiking days. Same trail, ends Sunday — but Sunday exit goes via Bolungarvík (no direct Hesteyri → Ísafjörður on weekends).

Drop-off: Sat 11 Jul · Sjóferðir 09:00 · Ísafjörður → Veiðileysufjörður
Pickup: Sun 19 Jul · HornstrandirTours · Hesteyri → Bolungarvík + ~20 min shuttle/taxi → Ísafjörður

Boat leaves Bolungarvík 09:00 to Hesteyri, returns mid-morning. Confirm exact return time when booking. Then ~20 min shuttle/taxi from Bolungarvík to Ísafjörður (~15 km, paved tunnel road).

Logistics

Booking now

  • Sjóferðir Ísafjörður→Veiðileysu Sat 09:00 — book@sjoferdir.is or westtours.is
  • Borea Hesteyri→Ísafjörður Mon 09:00 — info@borea.is · +354 456 3322
  • ~14,900 ISK / leg / person ≈ €100
  • Camp sites are free, no booking (Environment Agency)
  • Book 2 months ahead — peak July fills fast

Travel to Ísafjörður

  • Cologne → KEF → RKV (Reykjavík city) → Ísafjörður
  • Domestic flight ~40 min, frequent weather cancellations
  • Bus alternative ~6 h via Strætó
  • Build buffer day on front end

Pack list (subarctic, July)

  • 4-season tent (wind, not cold, is the killer)
  • 0 °C-rated bag · Goretex shell · insulated layer
  • Trekking poles · knee-high gaiters
  • Microspikes (mid-July snow on passes)
  • River-crossing footwear (neoprene + crocs)
  • Garmin inReach Mini — strongly recommended
  • Vestfirðir 1:100,000 hiking map (Ferðakort)

Food & fuel

  • 7 days food, ~3,500–4,000 kcal/day
  • Gas canisters can't fly — buy in Ísafjörður (N1/Olís) or Reykjavík
  • ~3 × 230 g screw-on canisters for two
  • Doctor's House at Hesteyri sells coffee/waffles/soup

Permits & rules

  • No permit needed for July visits
  • Camp only at designated sites (max 1 week each)
  • Drones: forbidden without Environment Agency permit
  • Campfires: forbidden everywhere
  • Stay 40 m from arctic fox / nesting birds
  • Travel-plan registration at safetravel.is recommended

Weather & bail-outs

  • Vedur.is is the authoritative forecast
  • If pass visibility <100 m → wait at base camp
  • Hornbjargsviti lighthouse hut (paid bunks late Jun–Aug)
  • Hesteyri Doctor's House (paid bunks)
  • Borea + Sjóferðir run Hesteyri Mon–Fri — usually <48 h delay
