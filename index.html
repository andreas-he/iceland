★ Option A — recommended

8 hiking days. Direct ferry both ways. Mon morning return enables same-day flight home.

Drop-off: Sun 12 Jul · Borea 09:00 · Ísafjörður → Veiðileysufjörður
Pickup: Mon 20 Jul · Borea 09:00 · Hesteyri → Ísafjörður (earliest — fits domestic flight)

Day-by-day

Sat 11 Jul — Travel in
Frankfurt/Amsterdam → KEF → RKV → Ísafjörður · guesthouse

Arrive Ísafjörður Sat evening. Buy gas canisters at N1 (24/7). Register travel plan at safetravel.is. Pack-out and sleep early — 09:00 ferry next morning.

Day 1 · Sun 12 Jul — Veiðileysufjörður → Hornvík
11 km5–7 hHafnarskarð ~400 m

Borea 09:00 to Veiðileysu. Cross Hafnarskarð (steep snow chute on north side mid-July). Camp at Hornvík (Höfn) — flush toilets, ranger station.

Day 2 · Mon 13 Jul — Hornbjarg cliff loop
~16 km6–7 hKálfatindar 534 m

Day-pack only. Puffins, gannets, fulmars on the dramatic edge. Camp Hornvík.

Day 3 · Tue 14 Jul — Hornvík → Hlöðuvík
14–15 km7–9 hAtlaskarð + Skálarkambur

Two passes, two river fords, "the heart of Hornstrandir" view. Camp Við Hlöðuvíkurós (pyramid dry toilet).

Day 4 · Wed 15 Jul — Rest at Hlöðuvík
flex

Coastal walk, Arctic fox spotting, swim at the black-sand beach.

Day 5 · Thu 16 Jul — Hlöðuvík → Hesteyri
14–15 km6–8 hKjaransvíkurskarð 426 m

Ford Kjaransvíkurá at the shoreline; another snowfield on descent. Camp Hesteyri — toilets, sink, ranger.

Day 6 · Fri 17 Jul — Hesteyri rest day
0–6 km

Doctor's House (Læknishúsið) cafe — waffles, soup, coffee. Walk to Stakkadalur whaling station ruins.

Day 7 · Sat 18 Jul — Sléttuhlíð / weather buffer
flex

Sléttuhlíð side trail. Built-in slack for ferry weather cancellations.

Day 8 · Sun 19 Jul — Final day at Hesteyri
camp

No Sunday ferry to Ísafjörður — so one more night at Hesteyri. Pack out for the trip home.

Mon 20 Jul — Ferry out + fly home
Borea 09:00 · Hesteyri → Ísafjörður · domestic flight → KEF → Frankfurt/Amsterdam

Borea 09:00 is the only option that fits a same-day flight home (Borea 16:00 or Sjóferðir 14:00 would force an overnight in Ísafjörður).

Why A is recommended

A and B have equal hiking days (8). A finishes with a direct Mon morning ferry to Ísafjörður — cleanest exit, one operator, one booking. B requires a Fri evening arrival (more buffer, but Fri flights typically cost more) and a Sunday transfer via Bolungarvík (two operators, an extra leg). C is the shortest version with the same Bolungarvík complications as B.

Logistics

Booking now

  • Drop ferry · A+C: Borea Sun 09:00 · B: Sjóferðir Sat 09:00 — book@sjoferdir.is or westtours.is
  • Pickup · A: Borea Mon 09:00 — info@borea.is · B+C: HornstrandirTours Sun — haukur@hornstrandaferdir.is · +354 862 2221
  • ~14,900 ISK / leg / person ≈ €100
  • Camp sites are free, no booking (Environment Agency)
  • Book 2 months ahead — peak July fills fast

Travel to Ísafjörður

  • Frankfurt/Amsterdam → KEF (direct ~3 h)
  • KEF → RKV (Reykjavík city) by bus/taxi ~50 km
  • RKV → Ísafjörður domestic flight ~40 min, frequent weather cancellations
  • Bus alternative ~6 h via Strætó

Pack list (subarctic, July)

  • 4-season tent (wind, not cold, is the killer)
  • 0 °C-rated bag · Goretex shell · insulated layer
  • Trekking poles · knee-high gaiters
  • Microspikes (mid-July snow on passes)
  • River-crossing footwear (neoprene + crocs)
  • Garmin inReach Mini — strongly recommended
  • Vestfirðir 1:100,000 hiking map (Ferðakort)

Food & fuel

  • 7–8 days food, ~3,500–4,000 kcal/day
  • Gas canisters can't fly — buy in Ísafjörður (N1/Olís) or Reykjavík
  • ~3 × 230 g screw-on canisters for two
  • Doctor's House at Hesteyri sells coffee/waffles/soup

Permits & rules

  • No permit needed for July visits
  • Camp only at designated sites (max 1 week each)
  • Drones: forbidden without Environment Agency permit
  • Campfires: forbidden everywhere
  • Stay 40 m from arctic fox / nesting birds
  • Travel-plan registration at safetravel.is recommended

Weather & bail-outs

  • Vedur.is is the authoritative forecast
  • If pass visibility <100 m → wait at base camp
  • Hornbjargsviti lighthouse hut (paid bunks late Jun–Aug)
  • Hesteyri Doctor's House (paid bunks)
  • Borea + Sjóferðir run Hesteyri Mon–Fri — usually <48 h delay
